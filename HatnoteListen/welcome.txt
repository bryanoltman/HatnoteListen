Welcome!
	This app shows edits to Wikipedia as they happen, so you can watch as the encyclopedia grows and changes.
	Scroll down for an explanation of what everything means.
 
Each bubble represents a change to Wikipedia that was just made
 
You can tap on the bubble to get more information about its article
 
Bigger bubbles represent bigger changes
 
Light bubbles represent addition and make a bell sound
 
Dark bubbles represent subtraction and make a string sound
 
A white circle represents a change made by a registered user
 
Green circles are anonymous edits
 
Purple circles represent edits made by bots
 
New users are welcomed with a blue banner at the top of the screen
 
That's all!
